Vegetarian Tacos

Ingredients

  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1 can black beans, drained
  • 1 cup corn
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • Sour cream and salsa for serving

Instructions

  1. Warm tortillas in a pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix black beans, corn, avocado, and tomato.
  3. Place the mixture on each tortilla and top with cheese.
  4. Serve with sour cream and salsa.
